"LA VIDA ES UN CARNIVAL" - Listed among Rolling Stones list of the 500 Greatest Songs of All Time and Tropical Song of the Year for the Lo Nuestro Awards, this song is among Cruz’s most popular and influential. In this song, Cruz talks about how you should sing through life's struggles and hardships instead of crying as it's all just a carnival.

LA VIDA ES UN CARNIVAL VIDEO


"CELIA Y JOHNNY" - This album is one of if not Celia’s most famous albums in her career. It was so prominent it was enlisted to the National Recording Registry and part of Billboard’s "50 Essential Latin Albums of the Last 50 Years". This album contains songs such as “Toro Mata” and “Quimbara” (which will be mentioned later).

VIDEO FOR TORO MATA


“QUIMBARA” - This song is one of the most celebrated in Cruz's career and is said to have a huge impact on the salsa genre. She sang this song in a duet with Johnny Pacheco. Notably, the song title is also its hook which in English to… nothing. Quimbara is a made up term and was used for the rhythm of the song.

VIDEO FOR QUIMBARA


"YO VIVRE" -Yo Vivre - This song is a cover of Gloria Gaynor’s “I Will Survive”. Cruz partaking in this song, which is one that is seen as an anthem for the LGBT community, implies a lot on her stance on the matter. But unlike the original version, it is sung in Spanish.
