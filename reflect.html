Personal Reflection
Note: All elements including background images were created by me, Curtis Grimsley II
Celia Cruz meant a lot to me growing up, particularly for the song “La Vida Es Un Carnaval”. It was my gateway into Spanish culture and led me to listen to various Spanish songs and artists such as Selina and Becky G among others. I particularly remember dancing to it along with my friends in preschool, some of which were of Spanish descent. Through the song, it helped me bond closure to them and fed my desire for getting into Spanish culture. Every now and then, I take the time to learn a few Spanish terms and sentences to inch closer to an efficient overall understanding of the language. The better I am at communicating in Spanish, the more I can interact in Spanish speaking situations. I view Ms Celia Cruz as a starting point in my fixation on the ways and products of Latin America but it also doesn’t hurt that she lived in my home state New Jersey as well! I have a strong level of respect for people who come from the same area as me and I used to take a huge inspiration from celebrities who did. And Celia Cruz is no different.
